About Holic Thai Bistro

Our heritage is all about putting together equality, imagination, and culinary expertise. Holic Thai Bistro is the finest Thai restaurant ever roamed the Brooklyn region. By mixing different ingredients and cultures, we have crafted our own tastes and recipes.

Boasting Signatures & Gourmet Specialties

We proudly boast signature dishes and gourmet specialties while rotating new dishes and old favorites on a daily basis. We use only the freshest ingredients in order to produce flavorful signature and gourmet specialties. On top of our already unique dishes, we also craft our own drinks for you to enjoy at our restaurant bar.
